Bachelor of Business Administration

To acquire the key competencies needed by all the BBA program provides a strong foundation in core business disciplines to acquire the key competencies needed by all. Our Intensive BBA program focuses on enhancing the ability to present own views in front of a large audience to communicate, analyze situations take decisions using quantitative and qualitative factors.

Bachelor of Business Administration Eligibility

Student who has passed 10+2 exam from any stream with English as passing subject and secured minimum 45% marks in 10+2 class.Admission is based on the entrance test. Usually there is an objective-type test for admission to B.B.A. to evaluate English language,quantitative aptitude and reasoning skills.

  • Under Graduate Aptitude Test score of more than 500
  • Cleared 10+12+1 level of education (1 year of University education)
  • Scored more than 70% marks in Class 10+2
  • British 'A' level qualification with minimum grade B

Foreign students, who want to pursue an undergraduate course in management, would have to pass the GCE "A" level or any other examination equivalent to +2 systems of education (12 years of schooling).

Bachelor of Business Administration Subjects:

  • Business Data Processing
  • Business Laws
  • Financial and Management Accounting
  • Business Mathematics and Statistics
  • Business Economics
  • Personnel Management and Industrial Relations
  • Principles of Management
  • Marketing Management
  • Production and Material Management
  • Introduction to Operations Research

Admission information

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED FOR BBA ADMISSION

  • Mark sheet and pass certificate of your Class X or XII examination.
  • Proof of date of birth.
  • School leaving certificate
  • Transfer certificate
  • Domicile certificate/ residential proof or certificate
  • Provisional certificate
  • Character certificate
  • Scheduled Caste/ Scheduled Tribe/Other Backward Caste certificates
  • Proof of disability (if any)
  • Migration Certificate
